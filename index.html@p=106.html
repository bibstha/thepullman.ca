The Neighbourhood

At the centre of everything

At the centre of everything

The Pullman Coach Flats are located central to many of Ottawa’s most desirable, diverse, and culturally significant communities. At the heart of the city in an up-and-coming neighbourhood and within easy walking distance to everything in the city’s heart, The Pullman is also just a minute to the 417 and all points east and west.

To the west, in Little Italy, you can sample the best pasta and cultural events this side of the Amalfi Coast, while a few short blocks to the north, you can enjoy dim sum or Vietnamese cuisine in Chinatown. To the south, it’s a 15-minute walk to Lansdowne Park to take in a REDBLACKS, Fury or 67’s home game and dinner at one of the Glebe’s great bistros.

For those who work in Uppertown, along Elgin Street or Bank Street, a comfortable stroll will bring you past boutiques, bistros and cafés, and home to the The Pullman.

THE LOCAL SCENE

The central location of The Pullman ensures that life’s daily necessities are just a stone’s throw away

The Local Scene

Charlesfort’s reputation for excellent design and construction, and attention to detail in the finishes, ensure that The Pullman will set the tone for redevelopment in the neighbourhood, while residents of The Pullman will enjoy the hip vibe of this Ottawa area that is so rich in diversity.

The central location of The Pullman ensur es that life’s daily necessities are just a stone’s throw away: grocery stores Massine’s Independent and Herb & Spice are conveniently located within a few blocks; superb ready-made foods at The Red Apron and True Loaf Bakery are available just down the street; and loads of great restaurants abound nearby—such as The Whalesbone, Fauna, Wilf and Ada’s—to name only a few.

MacNabb Recreation Centre and Jack Purcell Community Centre offer recreational programs for all ages, and young families will enjoy the proximity of local elementary schools Centennial, Elgin Street and Glashan.
